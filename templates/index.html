In congruence with our value in environmental stweardship, our group decided to focus on honeybee populations as a critical topic worthy of exploration. Honeybees are an example of what are considered keystone species that play crucial roles in maintaining the integrity of ecosystems. In the case of honeybees, they are responsible for the continued reproduction of plants via pollination--a function essential for agriculture and human food supply.

Our project vusualizes bee colony data over time, tracking number the number of colonies and beekeepers per state as well as known causes of colony loss--a subject of great importance to researchers in the field.
